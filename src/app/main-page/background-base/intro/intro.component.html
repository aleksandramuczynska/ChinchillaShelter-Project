About our Shelter

Our Shelter was estabilished in 1990 by Charlie Chinchill, by our Founder who decided to make lives of smaller and weaker creatures better. He's always had the love and affection for chinchillas, which weren't a popular pet choice. Since then we've been searching for all those lost, tiny souls while also trying to find them a warm and loving home. We hope to make this world a better place for our tiny friends. That's how we became the largest shelter for chinchillas in the whole country.
Since it's become our mission, we've found homes for over 4000 chinchillas. Every week we get at least one new chinchilla to care for, which isn't a lot, unless nobody wants to adopt them. That's why our mission is so important. We hope we've conveyed our wholehearted feelings and purpose of our organisation.
